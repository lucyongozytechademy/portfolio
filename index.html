About Me

Welcome to My Creative World! I'm Lucy Wanjiru Njoroge — a passionate journalist, writer, video editor, graphic designer and Data Analyst based in Kenya.
With a sharp eye for detail and a love for storytelling through words and visuals, I craft compelling narratives that inform, inspire, and influence.

I am a multimedia storyteller who thrives at the intersection of journalism, visual media, and community-based research.
With a background in journalism and criminology and years of field experience, I transform complex issues into engaging and accessible content. My creative and analytical approach allows me to communicate ideas powerfully through videos, graphics, and written reports.

Whether capturing voices in rural communities or visualizing data for non-profits, my mission is to amplify real stories that matter. I am fluent in English and Kiswahili, with working knowledge of French and Kikuyu, which enables me to connect across diverse audiences.

I bring a unique blend of journalistic inquiry and multimedia design, backed by years of hands-on experience working on development, humanitarian, and research-driven projects.
I have collaborated with leading organizations including Ongozy Technology, World Vision Kenya, Partnership for Economic Policy (PEP), and the International Potato Centre (CIP).

Through these partnerships, I have documented real-world impact, captured human stories, and created compelling media that brings data,and communities to life.
